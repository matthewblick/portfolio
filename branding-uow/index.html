Branding the University of Worcester design course


After finishing my degree at the University of Worcester, I was brought back in to produce a set of promotional items, branding the Graphic Design & Multimedia course to prospective students.

 

The project resulted in the creation of posters and hand-outs, that appeared on open days at the university's, higher education fairs, and similar events. A pack of three, respective of each year of the course, provides descriptive information about the different modules available, accompanied by a timeline to illustrate how the semesters are structured.
