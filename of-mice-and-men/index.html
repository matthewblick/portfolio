Of Mice and Men book jacket


A re-designed book jacket of my favorite novel, 'Of Mice and Men', written by John Steinbeck.

 

If you have ever read the story you will know that Steinbeck's description of the landscape in each scene is very meticulous, allowing the reader to imagine the setting in great detail. This is what I wanted to convey behind the concept of the front cover. I therefore went on to develop an interesting view of the landscape, using images of mountains and lakes that I have photographed over recent years.

The abstract image that I finalised was also used on a poster, along side a quote from the book that I displayed at my university degree show in May '14.
