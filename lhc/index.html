Large Hadron Collider guide book


A Concept for a guide to the Large Hadron Collider in Geneva, Switzerland.

 

This design takes strong International Typographic Style influence, creating a clean and crisp presentation. The cover uses an abstract take on colliding particles, with the inner pages displaying the information legibly and with style.
