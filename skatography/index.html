Skatography


"Skateboarding is not a hobby, and it's not a sport. Skateboarding is a way of learning to redefine the world around you." - Ian MacKaye

 

Skateboarding is a creative pursuit that will never stop evolving. There are no rules or referees to limit this; at least not in its traditional sense. Photographing skateboarding is in a way comparible to the publishing of writing. All the hard work and dedication it takes is engraved into a medium, that can be showcased to those it interests.
