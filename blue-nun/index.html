Blue Nun Wine


Re-introducing classic German wine, Blue Nun, with a new, au courant label.

 

Wine gets better with age, and so should it's identity, which is why drawing inspiration from international typographic design, as a timeless style, is a perfect fit. Illustrated using pencil and charcoal, the nun provides an image for use across the packaging design, from the wine box, to the label.
